Welcome to our guide to customizing your WooCommerce store using blocks, block themes, and full-site editing.

You’ll learn how to:

- Customize your shop page and product catalog.
- Use cart and checkout blocks for a conversion-optimized checkout experience.

Whether you’re looking to fine-tune your store’s templates, create a new design with blocks, or create custom product pages for specific products, we’ve got you covered. We aim to help you harness the full potential of WooCommerce, allowing you to craft a unique, efficient online store that meets your needs.
