This extension was previously referred to as **WooCommerce Services**.

- WooCommerce: version 3.0.0+
- WordPress: version 4.5+

[WooCommerce Shipping & Tax](https://woocommerce.com/products/shipping/) helps get your store ready to sell as quickly as possible. You create your products — we take care of tax calculation, payment processing setup, and shipping label printing!

#### Collect accurate taxes at checkout

Opt for Automated Taxes — tax rates are set up automatically; no need to enter them manually.

#### Print shipping labels for USPS and DHL at a discount

Offer lower rates on shipping. Create ready-to-print shipping labels for USPS and DHL directly in WooCommerce and take advantage of our special discount. Labels cannot be generated for Canada Post at this time.

1. Install WooCommerce Shipping & Tax .
  - First, visit WordPress.org to download [WooCommerce Shipping & Tax](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce-services/).
  - Next, in your store’s WP Admin dashboard, head to *Plugins > Add New > Upload Plugin > Choose File*. Select the .zip file you downloaded from WordPress.org and click **Install Now**.
  - Alternatively, head to*****Plugins > Add New*****in your store’s WP Admin dashboard and search for **WooCommerce Shipping & Tax**. Click the **Install Now** and **Activate** buttons.
  
2. Connect your site to WordPress.com :
  - Follow the prompt to connect your WordPress.com account (this is the [same account you use to log into WooCommerce.com](https://woocommerce.com/2017/01/woocommerce-requires-wordpress-account/)).
  - Once you are directed back to your site, a **Setup complete** banner will be displayed.
