The following extensions are supported by or add features to the WooCommerce Mobile App:

- **Composite Products** — read-only support added to iOS in version `13.2` and Android in `13.3`.
- [Gift Cards](https://woocommerce.com/products/gift-cards/)— Support in order creation/editing added to iOS in `15.6` and Android in `16.8`.
- [Min/Max Quantities](https://woocommerce.com/products/minmax-quantities/)— read-only support added to Android `13.2` and iOS `13.4`. Editing capabilities for Min/Max rules added to Andriod `19.2` and iOS `18.8`
- **Product Add-Ons** — currently an experimental (opt-in) feature only, added to iOS in `6.7` and Android in `7.5`.
- [Shipment Tracking](https://woocommerce.com/products/shipment-tracking/)
- [Product Bundles](https://woocommerce.com/products/product-bundles/)— Support in order creation/editing added to Android and iOS in `16.3`; read-only support for product details added to iOS in `12.8` and Android in `13.2`.
- **Stripe** — supported by our [In-Person Payments feature](https://woocommerce.com/document/stripe/admin-experience/in-person-payments/) as an alternative to WooCommerce Payments. IPP using the Stripe extension is only available in the US.
- **Subscriptions** — Support for creating/editing subscription products added to Android in `16.3` and iOS in `16.4`; read-only support in orders.
- **WooCommerce Shipping** — Support for [creating shipping labels for orders](https://woocommerce.com/document/creating-shipping-labels-in-the-mobile-apps/).
- [WooCommerce Payments for In-Person Payments](https://woocommerce.com/document/woopayments/in-person-payments/getting-started-with-in-person-payments/)– only available to merchants in the US, UK, and Canada.
- [Google for WooCommerce](https://woocommerce.com/products/google-listings-and-ads/) (Formerly Google Listings and Ads)- Added on `19.7` for iOS and `19.8` for Android

If you’re using the Jetpack plugin to connect the app to your site, make sure that the Jetpack connection is active and working correctly. You can verify some[known issues](https://jetpack.com/support/getting-started-with-jetpack/known-issues/)or try[reconnecting your site](https://jetpack.com/support/reconnecting-reinstalling-jetpack/).

If you’re still having difficulty, please fill out our contact form to reach our support team from within the app by going to Menu > Settings > Help & Support > Contact Support.
